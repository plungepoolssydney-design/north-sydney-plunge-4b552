How long does it take from ordering to having my plunge pool ready to use?
The typical timeline for a precast plunge pool installation in Manly Vale ranges from 4 to 8 weeks. After your initial consultation and site inspection, we finalize your design and order the precast shell. Once it arrives (usually within 2 to 3 weeks), installation—including excavation, crane placement, plumbing, and filling—generally takes another 2 to 3 weeks. Weather and site conditions can affect these timeframes, but we keep you updated throughout the process.
Are you fully licensed and insured to install plunge pools in NSW?
Yes, North Sydney Plunge Pools is fully licensed and insured for plunge pool installation in NSW. Our team holds all required NSW builder and pool installer licenses, and we carry comprehensive public liability insurance. We also handle all necessary council approvals and compliance checks specific to the Manly Vale area, ensuring your installation meets local regulations.
What is the process for installing a precast plunge pool on my property?
Our process starts with a free on-site assessment in Manly Vale, where we evaluate access, soil conditions, and pool placement. After finalizing your design and securing council approval, we prepare the site and schedule delivery of your precast pool. The pool shell is craned into place, followed by plumbing and electrical connections. We then backfill around the pool, install filtration, and complete finishing touches like coping and paving. After thorough testing, your pool is ready to fill and enjoy.
